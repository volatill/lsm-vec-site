What you get

A dedicated instance

Your own isolated LSM-Vec database, reachable over HTTPS with an API key — no infrastructure to manage.

Client + HTTP API

The dependency-free Python client (lsmvec-client) plus a plain REST API you can call from any language.

Hands-on help

We help you load your data and get your first queries running, and stay reachable while you evaluate.

Free for the pilot

No cost and no card during the pilot. We onboard partners by hand, so it's a conversation, not a signup funnel.

How to apply

1

Tell us about your workload

Fill in the pilot request form — who you are, what you want to build, and rough scale (how many vectors, what dimension).

2

We provision your instance

We set up a dedicated instance for your vector dimension and metric, and send you the endpoint and API key.

3

Connect and load your vectors

Install lsmvec-client, point it at your endpoint, and start inserting and searching. See the docs to get going.

What about pricing?

The pilot is free. When it ends, pricing will be flat monthly — no per-query, per-read, or per-vector meter, with generous fair-use limits rather than a usage counter. A disk-oriented design keeps our hosting costs low, so we don't need to charge by the operation. Talk to us for the plan that fits your workload.
